## FAQ
> *Why is your username `SupImDos`?*

:   My childhood nickname was "*Haydos*", which was often shortened to just
    "*Dos*". The username itself is a shortened and stylised form of the
    greeting "*Sup, I'm Dos*", which I copied from one of my friends who was a
    very high level on Runescape. I eventually used the username long enough
    that I got stuck with it.
